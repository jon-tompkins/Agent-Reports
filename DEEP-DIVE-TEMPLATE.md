## Rating Definitions

| Rating | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **STRONG BUY** | High conviction, significant upside (>30%), favorable risk/reward |
| **BUY** | Positive outlook, good upside (15-30%), acceptable risk |
| **HOLD** | Fair value, limited near-term catalysts, maintain existing position |
| **SELL** | Negative outlook, better opportunities elsewhere, reduce position |
| **STRONG SELL** | High conviction downside, significant risks, exit position |

## Risk Level Definitions

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Low** | Large cap, profitable, stable business, low volatility |
| **Medium** | Mid cap or growth stage, some execution risk |
| **High** | Small cap, unprofitable, high volatility, binary outcomes |
| **Speculative** | Micro cap, pre-revenue, or turnaround with significant uncertainty |
