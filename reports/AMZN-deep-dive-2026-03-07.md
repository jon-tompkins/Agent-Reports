## Investment Summary

### Rating Justification

Amazon earns a **BUY** at current levels after the post-capex pullback. The market's reaction to $200B capex was overdone — this is Amazon betting massively on AI infrastructure at exactly the right time. AWS reaccelerated to 24% growth (fastest in 13 quarters), advertising is a monster at $85B+ revenue, and retail margins hit records.

At 45x earnings, it's not cheap, but you're getting:
- The #1 cloud with accelerating growth
- A $85B advertising business growing 20%+
- Record retail efficiency
- Massive AI infrastructure optionality

The October FTC trial is a risk, but Amazon has navigated antitrust before. The capex cycle creates near-term FCF pressure but sets up 2027+ for potential margin expansion as AI workloads monetize.
