## Company Overview
AXT Inc is a materials-science company manufacturing compound semiconductor substrate wafers:
- **Indium Phosphide (InP)** ‚Äî data-center optics, 5G, fiber-optic lasers, detectors
- **Gallium Arsenide (GaAs)** ‚Äî RF amplifiers, Wi-Fi, satellite communications, VCSELs
- **Germanium (Ge)** ‚Äî solar cells, infrared detectors

**Key differentiator:** Proprietary vertical-gradient freeze (VGF) crystal-growth process produces high-purity, low-defect InP substrates ‚Äî superior to commodity-grade alternatives.

**Critical Note:** Manufacturing is in CHINA, making them subject to Chinese export controls.

## Export Control Risk (THE BIG ONE) üö®

### Timeline:
- **August 2023:** China imposed export controls on GaAs
- **February 2025:** China added InP to export control list
- **Current:** Every customer order requires government export permit

### Impact:
- Permits taking ~60 business days (~3 months) to process
- Customers adapting with longer lead-times and advance ordering
- Creates revenue recognition volatility
- **Q1 2026 risk:** US-China policy updates could materially impact permit processes

### Mitigants:
- Company has been successful obtaining permits so far
- Growing backlog suggests customers committed despite delays
- Customers placing longer-term orders for visibility
