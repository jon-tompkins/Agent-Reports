## Company Overview
Coherent Corp is a global leader in photonics, formed from the merger of II-VI and Coherent (2022). They provide:
- **Optical transceivers** (800G, 1.6T for AI data centers)
- **Silicon photonics** components
- **Indium Phosphide** substrates and lasers
- **Silicon Carbide (SiC)** for power electronics
- **Laser systems** for industrial/telecom applications

**Key differentiator:** Vertically integrated US-based manufacturing, CHIPS Act beneficiary, diversified across multiple photonics markets.
