## 1. Company Overview

### What They Do
Volatus Aerospace Inc. (formerly Drone Delivery Canada) is a leading provider of integrated drone solutions across North America, expanding into Latin America. The company operates through two main business divisions:

1. **Equipment Sales** - Drone systems, parts, and technology
2. **Aerial Services & Training** - BVLOS operations, inspections, medical deliveries, training programs

### Core Operations
- **Drone Delivery Services**: Medical supplies via Halton Healthcare partnership
- **Industrial Inspections**: Agriculture, construction, energy, pipeline monitoring
- **Defense & Security**: Surveillance, monitoring, Arctic operations
- **Training & Certification**: Professional drone operator programs
- **Equipment Distribution**: One of fastest-growing drone distributors in North America

### Key Infrastructure
- **Remote Operations Control Center (OCC)** in Toronto
- **Nationwide BVLOS Operations** approved by Transport Canada
- **24/7 operational capability** with qualified operators
- **Edmonton International Airport** operations base
- **Planned Innovation Centre** at Mirabel for defense readiness

### Recent Corporate Actions
- **August 30, 2024**: Completed merger with Volatus Aerospace Corp.
- **September 5, 2024**: Started trading as "Volatus Aerospace Inc."
- **Ticker symbols maintained**: TSXV: FLT, OTCQX: TAKOF, Frankfurt: A3DP5Y
