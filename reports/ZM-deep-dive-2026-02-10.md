# Zoom Video Communications (ZM) - Deep Dive Research Report
**Date:** February 10, 2026  
**Market Cap:** ~$27.3 billion  
**Current Price:** ~$94 (as of analysis date)  

---

## Executive Summary

Zoom Video Communications has evolved from a pandemic beneficiary into a diversified communications platform company. While facing post-pandemic growth normalization, the company has strategically pivoted toward AI integration, enterprise expansion, and strategic investments. **Most notably, their early $51M investment in Anthropic through Zoom Ventures could now be worth $2-4 billion**, representing one of the most successful corporate venture investments of the decade.

---

## 1. Business Overview

### Current State
- **Revenue (FY2025):** $4.81B, up 3.3% YoY in Q4
- **Market Position:** Dominant 57% market share in video conferencing vs Microsoft Teams (25%), Google Meet (6%), Cisco Webex (6%)
- **Business Model Evolution:** Transitioning from pure-play video conferencing to comprehensive communications platform

### Revenue Breakdown & Growth Trends
- **Basic subscription revenue** continues to decline as expected post-pandemic
- **Enterprise growth** remains steady but slower than pandemic peaks
- **Phone and Contact Center solutions** showing promise for diversification
- **Growth Rate:** Stabilized around 3-4% after dramatic pandemic surge and subsequent normalization

### Competitive Position
**Strengths:**
- Entrenched market leadership with 57% market share
- Superior user experience and reliability
- Strong enterprise adoption and switching costs
- Brand recognition as synonym for video calling

**Challenges:**
- Microsoft Teams bundling advantage with Office 365
- Google Meet's integration with Workspace
- Slower growth as hybrid work stabilizes
- Pricing pressure from competitors

**Investment Thesis:** Zoom represents a high-quality business trading at reasonable valuations with significant hidden value from strategic investments. The Anthropic investment alone provides substantial upside optionality, while the core business transitions from pure pandemic beneficiary to diversified communications platform.

**Key Value Drivers:**
1. **Anthropic investment** potentially worth $2-4B vs $51M cost
2. **AI monetization** driving ARPU expansion
3. **Platform diversification** reducing video-only dependency  
4. **Strong cash position** enabling strategic flexibility

**Risk/Reward:** Attractive asymmetric setup with limited downside (cash-rich, profitable) and multiple upside catalysts (AI value recognition, platform expansion, hidden assets).

**Recommendation:** Strong consideration for investors seeking exposure to AI/communications with defensive characteristics and hidden value catalysts.
