# AI Victims Trade Thesis Research

## Overview
Large-cap companies (>$10B market cap) with liquid options that are likely to become LESS relevant as AI adoption increases. These are potential short/put candidates.

## Research Categories
- Content/Creative: Stock photo, basic content, translation companies
- Professional Services: Consulting, legal, accounting firms
- EdTech: Traditional education models
- Customer Service: Call centers, support outsourcers
- Data Entry/Processing: Manual data work companies
- Traditional Software: Apps made obsolete by AI

## Already Identified
- ORCL (Oracle) - Legacy enterprise software
- DUOL (Duolingo) - AI tutoring threat
- ADBE (Adobe) - AI image generation disruption
