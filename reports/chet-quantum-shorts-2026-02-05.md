# Overbought Quantum Stocks: Short Position Candidates
*Research Report - February 5, 2026*

The AI/quantum hype has created a potential shorting opportunity as fundamentals fail to support current prices.

- Collective gains ranging from 43% to 545+ in 2025
- All companies remain deeply unprofitable with worsening losses
- Revenue growth minimal relative to market caps
