## 1. Company Overview

### Business Description
M-tron Industries, Inc. (MPTI) is a specialized designer and manufacturer of highly-engineered, high-reliability frequency and spectrum control products. Founded in 1965 and headquartered in Orlando, Florida, the company focuses on controlling frequency and timing of signals in electronic circuits.

### Core Products & Technologies
- **Radio Frequency Components:** RF, microwave, and millimeter wave filters
- **Oscillators:** High-performance OCXOs (Oven-Controlled Crystal Oscillators), TCXOs, VCXOs
- **Integrated Solutions:** Phase-locked loop OCXOs, low jitter oscillators, harsh environment solutions
- **Specialized Components:** Crystal resonators, integrated microwave assemblies (IMA), solid-state power amplifiers
- **Advanced Filters:** Cavity, crystal, ceramic, lumped element, and switched filters

### Markets Served
- **Primary:** Aerospace & Defense (dominant revenue driver)
- **Secondary:** Commercial avionics, space applications, industrial markets
- **Geography:** United States and international markets
