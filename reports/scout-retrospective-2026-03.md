## Lessons Learned

### What Worked
1. **Oversold bounces**: HOOD and EXPE bought on technical oversold conditions are recovering
2. **Energy infrastructure**: ET thesis on AI power demand validated by ongoing contract wins
3. **Short thesis on weak fundamentals**: BE short working as fuel cell economics continue to challenge

### What Didn't Work
1. **Shorting AI momentum**: MDB short got run over by continued AI enthusiasm
2. **Timing**: Some positions slow to develop (AMD short taking longer than expected)

### Process Improvements
1. **Avoid shorting AI-adjacent names** unless clear fundamental catalyst (not just valuation)
2. **Set tighter stops on shorts** — MDB should have been stopped at $380
3. **Add more conviction sizing** — winning calls had small size, should have sized up on high-conviction
4. **Track catalyst dates** — note earnings, product launches, regulatory events in each report
