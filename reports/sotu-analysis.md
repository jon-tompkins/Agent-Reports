# State of the Union 2026 - Investment Analysis Report
**Date:** February 25, 2026  
**Speech Date:** February 24, 2026  
**Duration:** Longest SOTU in television history

---

## Executive Summary

1. **Energy Bullish:** "Drill baby drill" policy continues - oil production up 600k bbl/day, gas at ATH, gasoline at $1.85-$2.30/gal. Venezuela deal adds 80M barrels. Refiners and producers positioned to benefit.

2. **Defense Spending Surge:** $1 trillion budget approved, $1.5 trillion proposed for FY2027 (+66% from 2026). NATO now at 5% GDP. Defense contractors major beneficiaries (LMT, RTX, NOC, GD, LHX).

3. **Healthcare Disruption:** "Great Healthcare Plan" aims to redirect insurance payments directly to consumers. Most-Favored-Nation drug pricing implemented via TrumpRx.gov. Headwind for managed care (UNH, CVS, CI), tailwind for pharma margins long-term.

4. **AI/Data Center Power:** "Ratepayer Protection Pledge" requires tech giants to build own power plants. Bullish for independent power producers (CEG, VST, NRG) and SMR nuclear plays (SMR, OKLO).

5. **Housing:** Executive order banning institutional investors from buying single-family homes. Negative for SFR REITs (INVH, AMH), positive for homebuilders (DHI, LEN, PHM).

- **Go long:** Defense (LMT, RTX), refiners (VLO, PSX), nuclear/power (CEG, SMR), homebuilders (DHI, LEN)
