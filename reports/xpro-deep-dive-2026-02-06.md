## Business Overview

Expro provides energy services spanning the entire well lifecycle:
- **Well Construction:** Tubular running, cementing, drilling, wellbore cleanup
- **Well Flow Management:** Testing, multiphase metering, fluid analysis
- **Well Intervention & Integrity:** Slickline, coiled tubing, wireline
- **Subsea Well Access:** Deepwater specialized services

**Geographic Segments:**
- North & Latin America (NLA) - largest revenue contributor
- Europe & Sub-Saharan Africa (ESSA)
- Middle East & North Africa (MENA)
- Asia-Pacific (APAC)
